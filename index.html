The Problem

Managing media files on
set can be chaotic.

Every shoot, the same headaches. Files go missing, no one knows who moved what, storage runs out without warning.

Files Get Lost

No confirmation, no audit trail. Files disappear and nobody knows where they went.

No Accountability

Who transferred what? When? From which card? Nobody can say for certain.

Storage Surprises

Drives fill up mid-shoot with no warning. You discover it too late.

Scattered Logs

Notes on phones, scraps of paper, voice memos. No single source of truth.

The Solution

Full visibility and control over
your media workflow.

MediaTrack puts every transfer, every crew member, and every gigabyte on one clean dashboard — in real time.

Transfer Tracking

Record every file transfer in real time — from which card, to which drive, by whom, and at exactly what time.

Crew Coordination

Assign crew roles, track activity per person, and keep everyone accountable with a clear audit trail.

Storage Management

Monitor capacity in real time. Get a clear view of used vs. available storage across all devices on set.
